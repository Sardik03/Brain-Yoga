OUR STORY - WHO WE ARE

Brain Yoga Classes is a customised open learning space designed for every child and adult. To help learners strengthen their thinking caps, academic foundation and natural curiosity. Our core focus is always on building strong numerical understanding, improving concentration, developing personality, and fostering confident learning habits in your children who become part of our Brain Yoga family.

At centers of Brain Yoga, learning is never limited to textbooks. Which is why our academy’s journey started with a question from our Founder Sumaan Rastogi - why even today, children study every day, pursuing tons of subjects, thousands of hours of coaching and preparations, yet are confused, distracted, and most importantly afraid of learning? This is where we realised it’s important to bridge this gap and create a fun environment for students where they can study and learn enjoyably.

This is why you can find students of Brain Yoga Classes always encouraged to understand new concepts, ask questions, and brainstorm their own ideas. Unlike many traditional tuition centres, all our programs combine structured learning with practical ground activities that help children develop skills that support their growth in school and in everyday life. Even at the daycare level, children are guided to develop healthy habits, interact with others, and stay engaged in activities that keep their minds and bodies active.

The sessions at Brain Yoga are planned to keep the mind actively engaged in a balanced way. With consistent training and carefully guided exercises, students gradually build mental stamina, sharper focus, and a more natural flow of thinking while working. This process also strengthens coordination, improves the way they plan their work, and develops the habit of completing tasks with patience and accuracy. In a supportive environment with personal attention, children learn to handle challenges with greater stability and clarity.

OUR IDEOLOGY

The ideology of Brain Yoga Classes comes from years of observing how the way children learn and grow has changed with time. At Brain Yoga, we believe that education should not only prepare students for exams, but also help them understand the world around them and know they always have a place in it. This is the thought foundation on which Brain Yoga was built and still applies.

Another change that can be seen clearly is the growing distance between children and real-life experiences. Today’s generation of kids is surrounded by technology from the moment they are born. Mobile phones, computers/laptops, artificial intelligence, gaming devices and social media have made our lives a lot easier, but have also reduced the habit of thinking or using our brains. Earlier, children spent time playing outside, talking to friends, observing nature, and learning through everyday situations. Now, students look for shortcuts instead of understanding the logic behind. They want quick answers, ready-made solutions, and fast results, which slowly takes away their patience in learning.

Which is why our concept at Brain Yoga is simple: we believe that learning should not be limited to books, devices, or fixed career choices. Every child should get the chance to explore and understand how things actually work instead of only memorising what is written. Which is why your child’s activeness is 100% guaranteed as they get involved and connected with real experiences. Students are encouraged to participate in group activities, creative tasks, storytelling, debates, quizzes, discussions and many more activities. They learn to compete, interact, and develop scholastic and co-scholastic skills that support their overall growth.

MEET THE FOUNDER

Sumaan Rastogi

Founder, Brain Yoga Classes

At Brain Yoga Classes, we believe every child is unique. Each child has a different way of learning and understanding, and our founder, Sumaan Rastogi, strongly believes in this approach. Education should never follow only one fixed method, because every student has different abilities, interests and hidden talents. Children need the right environment where they can grow, learn and express themselves with confidence.

Sumaan Rastogi has more than two decades of teaching experience. She has worked with students of different age groups and learning levels, which has helped her understand that every child needs a different approach. Her focus has always been on strong basics, clear understanding and steady improvement. She believes learning should go beyond books and classrooms, because academics alone are not enough for a child’s growth. Confidence, discipline and independent thinking are equally important.

Her teaching method focuses on simple and practical learning. Students are encouraged to understand concepts clearly instead of memorising without meaning. Learning becomes easier when children enjoy the process and feel comfortable in class. Subjects like Mathematics, Hindi and other academic areas should not feel difficult or frightening. With the right guidance, every subject can become interesting and useful in daily life. Students should be able to connect what they study with real situations around them.

Brain Yoga Classes follow the idea that learning should be enjoyable as well as meaningful. Practice, activities and skill-based training are given equal importance along with academics. Children are encouraged to discover their strengths, improve their skills and develop confidence step by step. Her ideology is simple: education should reach every child in some way or another. Every student should get the opportunity to learn comfortably, without fear or pressure.

She strongly believes along with studies, students must develop confidence and courage. They should be able to face situations calmly and think clearly before reacting. At Brain Yoga Classes, the effort is not only to teach subjects, but to help children become comfortable with who they are. Students are guided to express their thoughts openly and to believe in their own abilities. A positive and supportive environment helps them grow with confidence.

Her aim is not only academic success, but the overall development of every child at the center. Learning should prepare them for real life, not only for exams. Her long-term vision is to expand Brain Yoga Classes into a wider learning space where education connects with creativity, nature and real experiences. She believes children learn best when they observe, explore and experience the world around them. This belief continues to guide Brain Yoga Classes every day, and remains the foundation on which the institute stands.
